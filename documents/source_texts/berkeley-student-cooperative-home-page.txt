Berkeley Student Cooperative home page
https://bsc.coop/

The Berkeley Student Cooperative is a 501c3 nonprofit housing cooperative founded in 1933.

The site highlights membership information, the member portal, vacancies for spring and summer, our houses and apartments, rates and availability, and the co-op mission of providing low-cost, community-oriented housing for students.

It also points to jobs, social media, the policy site, workshift site, and manager resources.

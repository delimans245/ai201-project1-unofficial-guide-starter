UC Berkeley Off-Campus Rental Services
https://och.berkeley.edu/

Off-campus rental housing listings and services for students, faculty, and staff.

Use the site to search for housing and roommates and to see what's nearby. The office can be reached at (510) 643-6544 or homeinfo@berkeley.edu.

The site also points users to landlord complaint reporting, office hours and location, ASUC housing tips, help center resources, and contact information for rental support.

Avoid Scams & Fraud
https://och.berkeley.edu/avoid-scams-and-fraud

Common scam red flags include rent offered below market, requests to wire money, requests to use another listing service or escrow account to transfer money, claims of affiliation with the site, dramatic landlord stories, and landlords who cannot or will not meet in person.

If you suspect a scam, report it immediately with as much detail as possible. The site recommends contacting the listing service, local police, the Internet Crime Complaint Center, and the Federal Trade Commission.

Roommate safety tips include limiting what you share online, meeting in public places, talking by phone before meeting, using privacy settings, trusting your instincts, and never giving out banking information.

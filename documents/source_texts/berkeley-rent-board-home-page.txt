Berkeley Rent Board home page
https://rentboard.berkeleyca.gov/

Latest news, featured events, and tenant resources for Berkeley renters.

The site highlights registration fees, petition forms, rent ceiling guidance, and shelter plus care notices. It also provides counseling, meetings, committees, records online, news, events, and contact information.

The home page is a starting point for people looking for tenant-rights guidance, city housing services, and ways to connect with the rent board office.

BSC Academic Year Rates
https://bsc.coop/housing/academic-year-rates
The 2026-2027 academic year contract runs from August 21, 2026 to May 15, 2027.
For room and board houses, the cost is $4,986.00 per semester for Fall 2026 / Spring 2027. The cost includes food, utilities, cleaning supplies, toilet paper, furniture, a rich social environment, workshops, and other amenities. Hillegass Parker has larger single rooms at $5,895.00 per semester.
The apartments do not include food service and have different rates depending on bedroom count and complex. The page also lists payment schedules, cancellation deadlines, per-month estimates, and notes about Winter Break and deferred payment.
The payment schedule for Fall 2026 is split into three parts: one payment due with the contract, one on September 9, and one on October 7. Spring 2027 uses the same three-installment structure with payments due December 2, February 3, and March 3. If members stay through winter break, they do not incur additional charges for that time.
The cancellation deadline for the full academic year is on or before July 8, 2026. A member who wants to cancel only the spring portion must do so on or before October 7, 2026. The page also explains that BSC does not send bills and that members are responsible for making sure payment reaches the office on time.
The room and board houses include standard singles, deluxe singles, doubles, triples, and other room types depending on the house. The apartments section covers studio, 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, 3-bedroom, and 4-bedroom options with different totals and monthly estimates. Some utility costs are covered by the BSC, while some apartment residents may pay electricity or utility overages depending on the complex.
